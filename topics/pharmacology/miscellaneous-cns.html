DRUGS FOR ALZHEIMER'S DISEASE

INTRODUCTION

Alzheimer’s disease is a degenerative disease of the cerebral cortex associated with degeneration of the cerebral cortex leading to memory and cognitive impairment.
FLOWCHART: Degeneration of cerebral cortex -> ↓ Cholinergic transmission -> Memory and cognitive impairment

DRUGS USED IN ALZHEIMER’S DISEASE

CEREBROSELECTIVE ANTICHOLINESTERASES

DRUGS

  • Donepezil
  • Galantamine
  • Rivastigmine

MECHANISM OF ACTION

FLOWCHART: Anticholinesterase drugs -> Inhibit breakdown of acetylcholine -> ↑ Cholinergic transmission in brain -> Improvement in cognitive function

CLINICAL EFFECT

  • Produce symptomatic benefit in some patients
  • Improve cognition and memory to some extent

MEMANTINE

CLASS

  • NMDA receptor antagonist

MECHANISM OF ACTION

FLOWCHART: Memantine -> Blocks NMDA receptors -> Reduces glutamate-mediated excitotoxicity -> Neuroprotective effect

OTHER DRUGS USED

  • Ginkgo biloba
  • Memantine

DRUGS FOR AMYOTROPHIC LATERAL SCLEROSIS (ALS)

INTRODUCTION

Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS) is a neurodegenerative disease affecting motor neurons in the brain and spinal cord.
FLOWCHART: Motor neuron degeneration -> Muscle weakness -> Muscle wasting

RILUZOLE

MECHANISM OF ACTION

FLOWCHART: Riluzole -> ↓ Glutamate release from neurons -> Reduces excitotoxic neuronal damage -> Slows disease progression

CLINICAL ROLE

  • Slows progression of ALS

OTHER DRUGS USED IN ALS

BACLOFEN

MECHANISM

FLOWCHART: Baclofen -> GABA agonist -> Reduces muscle spasticity

TIZANIDINE

MECHANISM

FLOWCHART: Tizanidine -> α2 agonist -> Reduces muscle spasticity

CNS STIMULANTS

INTRODUCTION

  • CNS stimulants include:
    • Analeptics
    • Psychostimulants

ANALEPTICS

FLOWCHART: Analeptics -> Stimulate respiratory center -> Increase respiration

PSYCHOSTIMULANTS

FLOWCHART: Psychostimulants -> Stimulate CNS activity -> Increase alertness and attention

ATOMOXETINE

CLASSIFICATION

  • Norepinephrine reuptake inhibitor
  • Not a classical CNS stimulant

ROUTE OF ADMINISTRATION

  • Oral

MECHANISM OF ACTION

FLOWCHART: Atomoxetine -> Inhibits norepinephrine reuptake -> ↑ Norepinephrine levels in CNS -> Improved attention + Reduced hyperactivity

USES

  • Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)

EFFECTS

  • Improves ability to pay attention
  • Decreases hyperactivity

ADVERSE EFFECTS

  • Insomnia
  • Gastrointestinal side effects
  • Rarely suicidal ideas

OTHER CNS STIMULANTS

DRUGS

  • Picrotoxin
  • Pentylenetetrazol
  • Strychnine

IMPORTANT FEATURE

FLOWCHART: Picrotoxin Pentylenetetrazol Strychnine -> Potent CNS stimulation -> Convulsions
